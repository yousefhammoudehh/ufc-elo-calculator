Fighter Insights
Type a fighter name or ID and press Load. The dropdown suggests top matches as you type. • FI window: fights = last N fights; days = last N days. • Half‑life: recency decay in days (higher = longer memory). • Rates: display per-minute or per‑15 equivalents.
Form Index
Form Index measures recent over/under‑performance vs ELO expectation, decayed by recency.
Momentum Slope
Momentum is the trend of ELO over the last K fights; positive slope indicates upward trajectory.
Pace & Efficiency (per minute)
Rates normalize output per minute fought to fairly compare pace and efficiency across different fight lengths.
Strength of Schedule (recent)
Recent opponent ELO (mean/median and interquartile range) provides context for streaks.
Style Profile
Control share and phase mix (distance/clinch/ground) summarize style tendencies.
Quality Wins
Quality wins are victories over opponents at or above the chosen ELO threshold at fight time.
Opponent‑adjusted Plus/Minus
Own per‑minute metric minus the opponent’s typical allowed rate (windowed). Higher is better.
Consistency & Versatility
Consistency via SD/CV of recent outputs; Versatility via entropy of win methods (0–1).
Finishing Hazard & Durability
Empirical finish timing density (KO/Sub buckets) and durability (1 − finishes per 15 minutes).
